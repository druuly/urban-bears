About

Welcome to UrbanBEARS!

We are a student-led organization and an aspiring 501(c) non-profit organization aiming to increase participation and inspire the next generation of youth in the biomedical engineering and biomedical sciences. UrbanBEARS works to increase participation and expand access in underserved communities that lack valuable hands-on and interactive STEM opportunities. We strive to support all of our members by connecting them to biomedical research and engineering internships, providing volunteer opportunities, and most importantly: reaching our goal of implementing free and accessible STEM chapters in schools with interactive technologies relevant to the field.

UrbanBEARS is also committed to ensuring members receive the academic, career, and college success in STEM fields they deserve by fostering a community of young leaders passionate for biotechnology, healthcare, and engineering, and allowing people to discover endless opportunities to expand their STEM journey as BEARS.

As a community, we hope to expand our network to encompass students all over the globe and provide them with STEM, research, and learning opportunities intended to deepen their curiosity for learning in biomedical engineering fields or research. We aspire to create leaders from all of our members so they can go out and make their own, unique, and profound impact on their communities to inspire others and pursue their dreams.
