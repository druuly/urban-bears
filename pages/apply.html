Student Research

UrbanBEARS Mentorship Program

The BEARS Research Program is a rigorous and selective research program designed for passionate students in the biomedical field seeking an introduction to scientific research and to develop their own projects with real, meaningful impact. Students join a cohort of interns to collaborate or work independently on a research project of their choice and receive guidance from an experienced researcher. Culminating in a research project to be shared at a symposium at the end of the term.

BEARS Explorers

Built for passionate and curious middle and high schoolers interested in volunteering to spread scientific literacy across the globe by publishing popular science articles. Explorers gain access to writing workshops, explore their own STEM interests at their own pace, and receive feedback from experienced researchers, with no deadlines or requirements.
